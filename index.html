We found that team-spirit is the most essential part of a football team. When compromised, both the performance and enjoyment are at risk and might lead to the disbanding of the team. Our goal is to offer a service for teams to maintain their team-spirit when it cannot be upheld naturally.

SquadLink is a free digital platform for players and coaches that provides interactive team bonding challenges, activities, and tools to help football teams build trust, improve communication, and strengthen chemistry both on and off the field.
