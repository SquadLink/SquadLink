Information

SquadLink is a free digital platform for players and coaches that provides interactive team bonding challenges, activities, and tools to help football teams grow stronger together.

By building trust, improving communication, and strengthening team chemistry both on and off the field, squads can enhance their overall performance and play better as a unit.

Designed as a team-first experience, SquadLink encourages squads to engage in real-life bonding activities while tracking their progress and chemistry over time through the app.

The platform is accessible on both laptops and mobile devices, so teams can stay connected, no matter where they are; on the field, at home, or on the go.
