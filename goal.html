Our Goal

Making the sport we all love, football, even more fun through stronger team bonds.

What are we?

SquadLink is a free digital platform for players and coaches that provides interactive team bonding challenges, activities, and tools to help football teams grow stronger together.

By building trust, improving communication, and strengthening team chemistry both on and off the field, squads can enhance their overall performance and play better as a unit.

Designed as a team-first experience, SquadLink encourages squads to engage in real-life bonding activities while tracking their progress and chemistry over time through the app.

The platform is accessible on both laptops and mobile devices, so teams can stay connected, no matter where they are; on the field, at home, or on the go.

Our Values

Team Spirit

We as SquadLink think that a good team-spirit is essential to a great football team. This is why we stand for bringing your team-spirit to the next level. We, as SquadLink, will give you all the tools needed to elevate your team and win every game with the best team-spirit to help you.

Enjoyment

Fun, laughter and unforgettable moments are what makes football enjoyable. Whether you’re strategizing with your teammates, celebrating victories or just sharing the love of the game, enjoyment stands at the centre of it all. SquadLink not only enhances but focuses on the betterment of enjoyment by improving the bonds between players.

Motivation

Motivation is a crucial part of our everyday life. In life, school or sports. While football should always be an enjoyment, every player can struggle with finding the motivation at times. SquadLink is the perfect tool to help motivate teams by providing incentives. Just take that first step. We’ll help with the rest.

Collaboration

Football is all about collaboration. You need to collaborate as a team, and professional football clubs collaborate to scout and train the best players for each other. Football could never have been this awesome without collaboration. That's why we value collaboration with others as well. We want to keep football awesome for each other!
